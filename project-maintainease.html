Home Maintenance Platform

MaintainEase

MongoDBExpressReactNode.jsInfluxDBAWS S3Redux

MaintainEase is a home maintenance platform that helps homeowners stay on top of everything their property needs. You can log tasks, set recurring schedules, upload documents to the cloud, and see a full history of what's been done and when. I used InfluxDB for the time-series data since it's a natural fit for tracking events over time, and tuned the REST APIs to keep queries fast even at scale. I also built a full test suite with Jest that covers 85% of the codebase — it caught 12 real bugs before they ever made it to production.
