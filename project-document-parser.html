Document Parser

PythonFastAPISupabaseOpenAI APIFAISS

I built a tool that lets you drop in any document — a PDF, Word file, spreadsheet, or plain text — and ask it questions in plain English. It reads through the content, finds the most relevant parts, and gives you a grounded answer directly from your files. No more scrolling through pages looking for one piece of information. The system uses vector embeddings and semantic search to understand what you're asking, not just match keywords — and a fallback layer to reduce the chances of it making something up.
