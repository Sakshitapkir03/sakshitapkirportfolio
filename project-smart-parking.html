Smart Parking

CArduinoSensorsLinux

Smart Parking is an embedded systems project I built using Arduino microcontrollers and C. Physical sensors are placed at each parking slot to detect in real time whether a space is taken or free. That data feeds into an LED display that immediately updates to show drivers which slots are available as they enter the lot — no app, no manual check, just instant hardware feedback. Building it was hands-on all the way: writing low-level firmware, wiring up the components, and debugging until the whole system worked together reliably.
